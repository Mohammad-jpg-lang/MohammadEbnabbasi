Cyberpunk Plot

Cyberpunk 2077 is a video game by CDProjekt Red, the game has you play as V, either as Vincent or Valerie depending on what sex your character is. You grow up either from a corporate, street or nomad background and this will affect what dialogue options you will have in the game. V's job is to find purpose, and this is done by making a name for yourself and become one amongst legends, little does he know, life is not so simple and legends are not so legendary as he find himself in constant turmoil, fighting for the most precious thing known to man and trying to regain what he is losing each passing day... Why Cyberpunk 2077 ?

I chose Cyberpunk 2077 because the game made me experience strong emotions, I have always been a fan of the Cyberpunk genre and I really enjoyed this game, especially in my second playthrough, playing the expansion called Phantom Liberty, I completed every mission and attained all the achievements, I have over 140 hours of gameplay time and I do not forget a single second.
